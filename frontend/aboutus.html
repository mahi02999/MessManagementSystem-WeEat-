About WeEAT

WeEAT is a platform dedicated to providing students with the best mess facilities and meal options in their campus. Our mission is to ensure that students have access to healthy, affordable, and delicious meals that meet their diverse dietary needs.

We believe that good food can enhance the learning experience, and we strive to create a vibrant community around food. Join us on this culinary journey and discover the flavors that make campus life enjoyable!
